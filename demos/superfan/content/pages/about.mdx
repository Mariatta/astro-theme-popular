Hi. I'm Riley Moon: writer, vinyl collector, karaoke over-preparer, and a Darren Hayes superfan since a friend's older sister taped Affirmation for me in 1997. By day I have a perfectly normal job; by night I maintain a spreadsheet of b-sides with a tab called "feelings".

This site is where the fandom lives: news updates when something arrives in the mail, concert diaries when I travel, essays when the annual re-ranking urge strikes, and a [listening guide](/listening-guide/) for anyone who wants in.

I also do the occasional [panel or podcast](/events/) about fandom, nostalgia and pop history. If you run one, my inbox is open.
